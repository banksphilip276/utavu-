About us

Research that matters, innovation that lasts

Utavu Foundation is a Kenyan non-profit supporting homegrown research and social innovation. We exist to bridge the gap between an idea and its real-world impact — providing funding, mentorship, and networks to the next generation of African problem-solvers.

"Utavu" means to enable, to allow growth — because we believe every community already holds the seed of its own solution.
Research
Rigorous, context-sensitive inquiry into the challenges communities face.
Prototyping
Rapid, iterative development of solutions tested in the real world.
Scale
Spreading what works — across counties, countries, and contexts.
